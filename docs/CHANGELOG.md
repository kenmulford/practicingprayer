## What's New (since 1.1.0b44)

### New Features

- **Collections** — Organize your prayer cards into collections like "Family," "Ministry," or "Work." Your cards are grouped into collapsible sections on the Prayer Cards page. Cards without a collection appear under "Loose Cards."
- **Manage Collections** — Create, rename, and delete collections from a new management page. Deleting a collection lets you choose to keep the cards or remove them entirely.
- **Collection picker on cards** — When creating or editing a prayer card, choose which collection it belongs to.
- **Multi-select & move** — Long-press any prayer card to enter selection mode. Tap multiple cards, then move them all to a collection at once.
- **Archived collection** — A built-in "Archived" collection lets you tuck away cards you're no longer actively praying for without deleting them.

### Improvements

- **Smoother page transitions** — Navigating between screens no longer produces a brief flash.
- **Clearer Prayer Time buttons** — The "Answered" and "I'm done" buttons in Prayer Time are now properly sized pill buttons with legible text.
- **Reliable tag actions** — Edit and Delete buttons on the Tags page now use tap-to-reveal action chips instead of swipe gestures.

### Concise Play Store version

Organize your prayer cards with Collections! Group cards into folders like "Family" or "Work," long-press to multi-select and move cards between collections, and archive cards you're not actively praying for. Also: smoother transitions, clearer Prayer Time buttons, and more reliable tag actions.
