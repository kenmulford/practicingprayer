## What's New (since 1.0.4 build 24)

### New

- **Quick Add now works even with no prayer cards** — A "Quick Add" card is automatically created the first time you use Quick Add without any existing cards. Prayers land there until you organize them into your own cards.

### New

- **Custom reminder times** — Set a specific time of day for each prayer reminder instead of the fixed 9:00 AM default.
- **Weekly day selection** — Choose which day of the week you'd like to be reminded for weekly prayers.
- **Monthly day selection** — Choose which day of the month for monthly prayer reminders.
- **Default reminder time** — Set your preferred default reminder time in Settings, so new prayers start with your schedule.

### Improvements

- The Quick Add card appears at the top of your cards list and can't be accidentally deleted or renamed.
- Prayer frequency now shows the full schedule (e.g., "Weekly on Tuesday at 7:30 PM") instead of just the frequency name.
