## What's New (since 1.1.0b44)

### New Features

- **Collections** — Organize your prayer cards into collections like "Family," "Ministry," or "Work." Your cards are grouped into collapsible sections on the Prayer Cards page. Cards without a collection appear under "Loose Cards."
- **Manage Collections** — Create, rename, and delete collections from a new management page (accessible from Settings or the Prayer Cards toolbar). Deleting a collection lets you choose to keep the cards or remove them entirely.
- **Collection picker on cards** — When creating or editing a prayer card, choose which collection it belongs to. Cards default to "Loose Cards" if no collection is selected.
- **Multi-select & move** — Long-press any prayer card to enter selection mode. Tap multiple cards across sections, then move them all to a collection at once — perfect for organizing after an update.
- **Archived collection** — A built-in "Archived" collection lets you tuck away cards you're no longer actively praying for without deleting them.

### Bug Fixes

- **Collections stay tappable when collapsed** — Previously, collapsing all collections could freeze the screen and require an app restart. Collections now always respond to taps.
- **Empty collections are visible** — Creating a new collection with no cards now shows the collection header immediately, with a helpful hint to add cards.
- **System cards appear in the right place** — The Quick Add and Shared with me cards now correctly appear under the System collection on all devices, including those that upgraded from earlier versions.
- **Multi-select toolbar moved to top** — The "Move to…" toolbar during card selection now appears at the top of the screen where it's visible, instead of at the bottom where it was hidden by your hand.

### Improvements

- **Collection headers are easier to read** — Card count badges on collection headers are now bolder and better matched to the section title styling.
- **Clearer multi-select mode** — Collection headers are dimmed during card selection so your focus stays on the cards you're selecting.
- **Smoother page transitions** — Navigating between screens no longer produces a brief flash. Pages slide in cleanly without the white flicker that appeared when opening Settings pages and other detail screens.
- **Clearer Prayer Time buttons** — The "✓ Answered" and "I'm done" buttons in Prayer Time are now properly sized pill buttons with legible text. "✓ Answered" is highlighted in warm gold so it stands out as the primary action.
- **Reliable tag actions** — Edit and Delete buttons on the Tags page now use tap-to-reveal action chips instead of swipe gestures, matching the Prayer Cards page pattern. No more missed swipes on Android.
